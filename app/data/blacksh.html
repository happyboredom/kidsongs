Ba, baa, black sheep, Have you any wool? Yes sir, yes sir, Three bags full. One for the master, One for the dame, And one for the little boy Who lives down the lane. Baa, baa, black sheep, Have you any wool? Yes sir, yes sir, Three bags full. One to mend the jerseys one to mend the socks and one to mend the holes in the little girls' frocks. Baa, baa, black sheep, Have you any wool? Yes sir, yes sir, Three bags full.
